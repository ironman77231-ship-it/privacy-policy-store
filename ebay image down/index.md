# Privacy Policy for eBay Image Downloader

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how eBay Image Downloader (the Extension) handles information when you use its product image downloading, image search, webpage translation, screenshot translation, and related shopping-assistant features.

The Extension is an independent third-party tool and is not affiliated with, authorized by, sponsored by, or endorsed by eBay Inc., Alibaba, 1688, Amazon, or any other marketplace mentioned in the Extension.

## 1. Scope of the Extension

The Extension is designed to help users download product images from eBay product pages and use related shopping-assistant features such as image search, screenshot-based search, webpage translation, and screenshot translation.

Some permissions are broadly scoped so that user-invoked features can work on the page where the user chooses to use them. The Extension does not use this access to build a browsing-history profile or to sell user data.

## 2. Information We May Process

To provide the Extension's features, we may process the following categories of information:

**Product media and page content.**  
When you use image downloading or image search features, the Extension may read product images, image URLs, product titles, page text, and visible page elements needed to identify and download media from the current page.

**User-selected images or screenshots.**  
When you use screenshot search, screenshot translation, or image search features, the selected image, screenshot, or related page context may be processed to complete the requested action.

**Translation content.**  
When you request webpage translation or screenshot translation, the relevant text, selected content, screenshot text, and language context may be processed to return the translated result.

**Cookies and session-related information.**  
The Extension may use the cookies permission where necessary to make enabled features work with the commerce platform you are currently using, such as accessing page resources that require an active browser session. The Extension is not intended to collect passwords or use cookies for behavioral advertising.

**Settings and preferences.**  
Feature settings, language choices, and other preferences may be stored locally in your browser through Chrome storage.

**Technical information.**  
Limited diagnostic information, such as extension version, browser language, feature configuration, error details, and request timing, may be processed to operate, secure, and improve the Extension.

## 3. How Information Is Used

We use the information described above only to:

- provide product image downloading features requested by you;
- support image search and screenshot-based search features;
- provide webpage translation and screenshot translation features requested by you;
- preserve your settings and improve reliability, security, and support;
- diagnose technical issues and prevent abuse; and
- comply with applicable legal obligations.

## 4. Image Search, Translation, and Service Providers

When you use image search, screenshot search, webpage translation, or screenshot translation features, the content required for that request may be transmitted over HTTPS to extension-service endpoints operated by us or our service providers.

This processing is necessary to generate and return the requested result. Do not submit sensitive personal, financial, or confidential information unless you are comfortable with it being processed for the requested feature.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to data brokers or advertisers. We do not use product images, page content, screenshots, or translation content to create behavioral-advertising profiles. Service providers may process limited request and security logs only as necessary to deliver, protect, and maintain the service.

## 5. Permissions Justification

**activeTab and scripting:** Used to run the feature you invoke on the active page, including image downloading, image search, screenshot search, webpage translation, and screenshot translation.

**contextMenus:** Used to provide right-click shortcuts for image search, screenshot search, and translation features.

**storage:** Used to save local settings and feature preferences.

**cookies:** Used only where an enabled feature requires session-aware access to the commerce platform currently in use.

**alarms and notifications:** Used for scheduled extension tasks and user-requested notifications.

**declarativeNetRequest and declarativeNetRequestFeedback:** Used to manage extension-related network behavior and diagnose request issues.

**downloads:** Used only when you choose a feature that saves product images or other files to your device.

**host permissions:** Used to let enabled content features operate on supported pages where you request them.

## 6. Data Retention and Security

Preferences are stored locally until you change them, remove the Extension, or clear browser data.

Image search, translation, screenshot, and diagnostic data are retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to process the request, maintain security, resolve errors, and meet legal requirements.

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, including HTTPS for network communications. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

## 7. Your Choices

You can disable features, change settings, revoke optional permissions, clear extension storage, or uninstall the Extension at any time through Chrome.

You can also avoid using image search, screenshot, or translation features for content you do not want processed.

## 8. Affiliate Disclosure

The Extension may participate in affiliate programs. If you follow a qualifying link or make a qualifying purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate activity does not change the way we handle product images, screenshots, translation content, or browsing data. We do not use this information to create behavioral-advertising profiles.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the Extension, supported features, or applicable requirements change. The current version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
