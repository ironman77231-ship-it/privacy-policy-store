# Privacy Policy for AliWangWang Translate - Chat Translator for 1688

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AliWangWang Translate - Chat Translator for 1688 (the Extension) handles information when you use its chat translation, page translation, screenshot translation, and related shopping-assistant features. The Extension is an independent third-party tool and is not affiliated with, authorized by, sponsored by, or endorsed by 1688, Taobao, Alibaba, AliWangWang, or their affiliates.

## 1. Scope of the Extension

The Extension is designed primarily to help users translate chat messages and shopping-related content on 1688, Taobao, and similar commerce experiences. Some permissions are broadly scoped so that user-invoked features, such as screenshot translation and page translation, can work on the page you choose to use them on. The Extension does not use that access to build a browsing-history profile or to sell user data.

## 2. Information We May Process

To provide the Extension's features, we may process the following categories of information:

- **Chat and text content.** When automatic translation is enabled or you request a translation, the relevant message text, selected text, screenshot text, and the language context required to return a translation may be processed.
- **Page and product context.** The Extension may read page text, product titles, product identifiers, and other visible page elements needed to provide translation, image-search, or shopping-assistant features that you activate.
- **Cookies and session-related information.** The Extension may use the cookies permission where necessary to maintain an authenticated platform session or to make an enabled feature work with the commerce platform you are currently using. It is not intended to collect passwords or use cookies for behavioral advertising.
- **Settings and preferences.** Language choices, enabled features, and other preferences are stored locally in your browser through Chrome storage.
- **Technical information.** Limited diagnostic information, such as extension version, browser language, feature configuration, error details, and request timing, may be processed to operate, secure, and improve the Extension.

## 3. How Information Is Used

We use the information described above only to:

- translate incoming or outgoing chat messages and user-selected text;
- provide page, screenshot, and image-based translation features requested by you;
- operate shopping-assistant features you enable;
- preserve your preferences and improve reliability, security, and support; and
- comply with applicable legal obligations.

## 4. Translation Requests and Service Providers

When you use a translation feature, the text or image content required for that request may be transmitted over HTTPS to translation or extension-service endpoints operated by us or our service providers. This processing is necessary to generate and return the requested translation. Do not submit sensitive personal, financial, or confidential information unless you are comfortable with it being processed for the requested translation.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to data brokers or advertisers. We do not use chat content to create behavioral-advertising profiles. Service providers may process limited request and security logs only as necessary to deliver, protect, and maintain the service.

## 5. Permissions Justification

- **activeTab and scripting:** Used to run the feature you invoke on the active page, including chat, page, screenshot, and image translation.
- **contextMenus:** Used to provide right-click translation and image-search shortcuts.
- **storage:** Used to save local settings and feature preferences.
- **cookies:** Used only where an enabled feature requires session-aware access to the commerce platform currently in use.
- **alarms and notifications:** Used for scheduled extension tasks and user-requested notifications.
- **declarativeNetRequest and declarativeNetRequestFeedback:** Used to manage extension-related network behavior and diagnose request issues.
- **downloads (optional):** Used only when you choose a feature that saves a file to your device.
- **host permissions:** Used to let enabled content features operate on the page where you request them.

## 6. Data Retention and Security

Preferences are stored locally until you change them, remove the Extension, or clear browser data. Translation and diagnostic data are retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to process the request, maintain security, resolve errors, and meet legal requirements. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, including HTTPS for network communications, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

## 7. Your Choices

You can disable automatic translation, change language settings, revoke optional permissions, clear extension storage, or uninstall the Extension at any time through Chrome. You can also avoid using translation features for content you do not want processed.

## 8. Affiliate Disclosure

The Extension may participate in affiliate programs. If you follow a qualifying link or make a qualifying purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. Affiliate activity does not change the way we handle chat content or create behavioral-advertising profiles.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the Extension or applicable requirements change. The current version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
