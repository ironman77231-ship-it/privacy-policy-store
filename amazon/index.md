Privacy Policy for Amazon Price Tracker - Independent Third-Party Version
Last Updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our Chrome Extension ("the Extension") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding the data we access.

1. Scope of Data Access
The Extension is designed strictly for e-commerce productivity and price analysis. Our code is configured to only operate on authorized Amazon global domains, including but not limited to:

*.amazon.com, *.amazon.co.uk, *.amazon.co.jp, *.amazon.de, *.amazon.fr, *.amazon.es, *.amazon.it, *.amazon.ca, and other regional Amazon marketplaces.

We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit your browsing history, personal communications, or data from any websites outside of these specified e-commerce platforms.

2. Information Collection and Usage
To provide the price tracking features, we access the following information:

Product Price Data: We collect and process publicly available product prices, titles, and ASINs on Amazon pages to generate price history charts.

Price Alerts (Local): If you set a price drop notification, the data is stored locally in your browser to trigger alerts.

Authentication & Session (Cookies): We use the cookies permission strictly to maintain your session on Amazon to ensure that localized pricing (e.g., shipping estimates) is accurate.

Anonymous Analytics: We may collect anonymized, non-personally identifiable usage data to improve the performance of our tracking engine.

3. Permissions Justification

activeTab & Scripting: Required to extract price information from the current Amazon product page you are viewing.

Alarms & Notifications: Used to periodically check prices in the background and notify you of price drops.

Storage: Used to save your price tracking list and user settings locally.

DeclarativeNetRequest: Used to optimize network requests and ensure the extension loads price charts efficiently without slowing down your browser.
