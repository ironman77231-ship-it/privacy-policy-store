# Privacy Policy for Price History Tracker for Shopee - Independent Third-Party Version
**Last Updated: March 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how our Chrome Extension ("the Extension") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding the data we access.

### 1. Scope of Data Access
The Extension is designed strictly for e-commerce productivity and price analysis. Our code is configured to only operate on authorized Shopee global domains, including but not limited to:
* `*.shopee.com.my`, `*.shopee.tw`, `*.shopee.sg`, `*.shopee.ph`, `*.shopee.id`, `*.shopee.vn`, `*.shopee.th`, `*.shopee.com.br`, `*.shopee.cn`, and other regional Shopee marketplaces.

We **DO NOT** collect, store, or transmit your browsing history, personal communications, or data from any websites outside of these specified e-commerce platforms.

### 2. Information Collection and Usage
To provide the price tracking and shopping features, we access the following information:
* **Product Price Data:** We collect and process publicly available product prices, titles, and IDs on Shopee pages to generate price history charts.
* **Price Alerts (Local):** If you set a price drop notification, the data is stored locally in your browser via `chrome.storage` to trigger alerts.
* **Authentication & Session (Cookies):** We use the cookies permission strictly to maintain your session on Shopee to ensure that localized pricing and regional settings are accurate.
* **Affiliate Disclosure:** To support development, this extension participates in affiliate programs. We may earn a commission from qualifying purchases at no extra cost to you.

### 3. Permissions Justification
* **activeTab & Scripting:** Required to extract price information from the current Shopee product page you are viewing.
* **Alarms & Notifications:** Used to periodically check prices in the background and notify you of price drops.
* **Storage:** Used to save your price tracking list and user settings locally on your device.
* **ContextMenus:** Required to provide quick "Search by Image" and "Translate" shortcuts via the right-click menu.

### 4. Independent Third-Party Disclaimer
This Extension is an **independent third-party tool** and is not affiliated with, authorized, maintained, sponsored, or endorsed by Shopee, Sea Group, or any of its affiliates. 

### 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure
* **No Third-Party Sale:** We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third-party advertisers or data brokers.
* **Encryption:** All data transmitted for price synchronization or feature functionality is encrypted via HTTPS.
