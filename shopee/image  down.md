# Privacy Policy for Shopee Image Downloader - Independent Third-Party Version

This Privacy Policy describes how our Chrome Extension ("the Extension") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding the data we access.

### 1. Scope of Data Access
The Extension is designed strictly for e-commerce productivity, image management, and price analysis. Our code is configured to only operate on authorized Shopee global domains, including but not limited to:
* `*.shopee.com`, `*.shopee.com.my`, `*.shopee.tw`, `*.shopee.sg`, `*.shopee.ph`, `*.shopee.vn`, `*.shopee.co.th`, `*.shopee.com.br`, `*.shopee.com.id`, `*.shopee.com.mx`, `*.shopee.com.co`, `*.shopee.com.pe`, `*.shopee.com.ar`, and other regional Shopee marketplaces.

We **DO NOT** collect, store, or transmit your browsing history, personal communications, or data from any websites outside of these specified e-commerce platforms.

### 2. Information Collection and Usage
To provide the image downloading and shopping assistant features, we access the following information:
* **Product Media Data:** We access publicly available product images, videos, and descriptions on Shopee pages to facilitate local downloading.
* **Price & Product Metadata:** We process product titles and IDs to generate organized filenames for your downloads and to provide optional price history charts.
* **Authentication & Session (Cookies):** We use the cookies permission strictly to maintain your session on Shopee to ensure that high-resolution images are accessible for download.
* **Affiliate Disclosure:** To support development and maintenance, this extension participates in affiliate marketing programs. We may earn a commission from qualifying purchases at no extra cost to you.

### 3. Permissions Justification
* **Downloads:** Required to save Shopee product images and videos directly to your device's default download folder.
* **activeTab & Scripting:** Required to detect and extract image URLs from the specific Shopee product page you are currently viewing.
* **ContextMenus:** Provides quick right-click shortcuts for "Download Images," "Search by Image," and "Translation" features.
* **Storage:** Used to save your download preferences and tool settings locally on your device.
* **DeclarativeNetRequest:** Used to optimize network requests to ensure high-speed image fetching without compromising browser performance.

### 4. Independent Third-Party Disclaimer
This Extension is an **independent third-party tool** and is not affiliated with, authorized, maintained, sponsored, or endorsed by Shopee, Sea Group, or any of its affiliates. 

### 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure
* **No Third-Party Sale:** We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third-party advertisers or data brokers.
* **Local Processing:** Image extraction and file renaming are performed locally within your browser. No user-generated content is uploaded to our servers.
* **Encryption:** All communication with Shopee servers for data retrieval is conducted via secure HTTPS.
